Why SAM.gov is Essential

Universal Requirement

SAM.gov registration is mandatory for all federal contracting opportunities, regardless of industry or organization type. It's your gateway to billions in federal contracts.

Unique Entity Identifier

Obtain your UEI (Unique Entity Identifier) - the key that unlocks access to federal opportunities and maintains your active status in the system.

Downstream Industries

Many industries and opportunities stem from SAM.gov registration. Start here to access grants, subcontracting opportunities, and federal assistance programs.
